### Mock Data
- 50 records generated deterministically from arrays of:
  - 10 UEMS unidade names
  - 20 curso names  
  - 3 grau types
  - 5 possible conceitos
  - 5 possible anos
- Each of 10 unidades has exactly 5 courses = 50 total
- Deterministic via index math (no Math.random)

- useMemo for all filtering/sorting/pagination computations
- toast from sonner for copy feedback
- Dark theme: bg-surface, text-foreground, text-muted-lavender, lime/coral accents
- All text in Brazilian Portuguese
